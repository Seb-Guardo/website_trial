A shopping website is an online platform where consumers can browse and buy products or services. These websites typically offer a wide variety of items, ranging from clothing and electronics to homeware and groceries. Customers can search for products, compare prices and features, and make purchases directly on the site. Many shopping sites also provide customer reviews and ratings to help shoppers make informed decisions. Popular shopping websites include Amazon, eBay, and Walmart. E-commerce has become increasingly popular in recent years, with many people preferring to shop online due to convenience and competitive pricing.
